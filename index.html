What is Car Infotainment?

Car infotainment systems are modern multimedia devices integrated into vehicles to provide entertainment, navigation, and communication services. These systems include a range of features such as high-definition audio and video playback, GPS navigation, hands-free calling, voice commands, and smartphone integration.
